About

A proven model, aimed at a niche nobody's claimed.

Creator-side representation isn't a new idea — it's an established category in beauty, gaming and family content. Nobody had applied it to overlanding specifically. That's the gap Waypoint fills.

The model, in short

Think of it as tax software for creators.

You already know the part that's hard to teach: how to make content people watch. We handle the part that's just process — market knowledge, brand relationships and deal infrastructure — so you're not writing cold outreach emails between edits.

Why overlanding specifically

The audience is unusually well matched to sponsorship.

Overlanding content sits between road-trip vlogging and competitive off-roading: the vehicle build and the journey both matter, which is exactly why gear brands sponsor it so heavily — their product is the hero of half the video. That combination of engaged, long-form viewers and naturally product-forward content is hard to find elsewhere in automotive.

How we operate

A short list of things we won't compromise on.

Everything in writing

Every representation agreement and every brand contract is a written document before any filming or payment happens — never a verbal understanding.

You keep what's yours

AdSense, memberships, merchandise and affiliate income are never part of our commission, regardless of how a sponsorship conversation goes.

Commission only on closed deals

We don't charge retainers, setup fees or "exposure" arrangements. If we don't close a deal, we haven't earned anything.

Disclosure, done properly

Every sponsored deliverable we broker includes correct FTC/ASA-compliant disclosure, written into the contract rather than left to good intentions.

Where things stand

Newly launched, on purpose.

Waypoint Creator Group is a new agency built around an established representation model. We're intentionally starting with one niche — overlanding — instead of launching broad across automotive, because specificity is what makes a pitch credible to both creators and brands.
