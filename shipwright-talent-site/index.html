Why AI builder tools, why now

Build-along content already decides which AI tools people pick up.

Brand marketing is shifting toward the creators builders already trust. AI app builders and AI-first dev tools sit at the center of that shift, with almost no one representing the creators themselves.

Category

A proven model, an open niche

Creator-side talent representation is established in beauty, gaming and family content. Generalist tech influencer agencies exist too, but they work for brands, sourcing across dozens of verticals. None specializes in representing the creators who build real software on camera.

Audience

Builders who buy what they build with

Viewers of AI app-builder and vibe-coding content are actively evaluating tools to build with themselves, not just browsing. That combination of engagement and purchase intent is rare in a software niche this size.

Fit

Builder-tool brands are already spending

AI app builders, AI-first IDEs and agent automation platforms are running active growth budgets aimed at non-technical builders, the same audience watching build-along videos on YouTube.

How a deal actually moves

From first email to both of you getting paid.

The same pipeline every time. No surprises in the middle of a deal.

You reach out

Tell us about your channel, your audience and the kind of sponsorships that actually fit, by email. No commitment either way.

Representation agreement & media kit

A short, plain-language agreement, then we build a media kit from your own channel data: stats, audience and past work.

Brand introductions

We approach relevant brands directly, negotiate terms and rates on your behalf, and bring you only the deals worth your time.

Contract & delivery

Deliverables, approval windows and payment terms are set in writing before filming starts. You build the content; we manage the logistics.

Paid together

You're paid your agreed rate once the brand's funds clear. Our commission only ever comes out of a deal we closed.
