About

A proven representation model, applied to one niche.

Creator-side representation isn't a new idea. It's an established category in beauty, gaming and family content. This applies that same model specifically to AI app-builder and AI dev-tool creators, a niche few agencies focus on exclusively.

The model, in short

A pipeline for your sponsorships.

You already know the part that's hard to teach: how to build things people want to watch you build. We handle the part that's just process: market knowledge, brand relationships and deal infrastructure, so you're not writing cold outreach emails between builds.

Why AI builder tools specifically

The audience is unusually well matched to sponsorship.

A build-along video where a creator ships a real, working product with a tool is naturally product-forward content. The tool is often central to the video, which is part of why AI app-builder and AI dev-tool brands have been increasing spend on this kind of content. That combination of engaged, long-form viewers and product-forward format is uncommon elsewhere in software.

How we operate

A short list of things we won't compromise on.

Everything in writing

Every representation agreement and every brand contract is a written document before any filming or payment happens. Never a verbal understanding.

You keep what's yours

AdSense, memberships, merchandise and affiliate income are never part of our commission, regardless of how a sponsorship conversation goes.

Commission only on closed deals

We don't charge retainers, setup fees or exposure arrangements. If we don't close a deal, we haven't earned anything.

Disclosure, done properly

Every sponsored deliverable we broker includes correct FTC and ASA-compliant disclosure, written into the contract rather than left to good intentions.

Where things stand

Newly launched, on purpose.

We're building our founding roster of creators and brand partners. Early partners get direct access and help shape how we work.
